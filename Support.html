Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I use SwipeCalc?
    A: Simply swipe and tap the on-screen buttons to perform calculations. For detailed instructions, see the in-app help section.
  • Q: Does SwipeCalc store my data?
    A: No, all calculations are processed locally on your device. We do not collect or store any personal data.
  • Q: The app is not working as expected. What should I do?
    A: Please try restarting the app. If the issue persists, contact us with details about your device and iOS version.
